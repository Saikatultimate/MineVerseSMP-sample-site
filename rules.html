Server Rules

Please read these rules carefully. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse for breaking them.

General Conduct

  • 1.1Be respectful to all players and staff members. Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.High
  • 1.2Offensive usernames, skins, or builds are prohibited. You will be asked to change them or face a ban.Medium
  • 1.3Impersonating staff members or other players is strictly forbidden.High

Chat & Communication

  • 2.1No spamming, flooding, or repeating messages excessively.Medium
  • 2.2Advertising other Minecraft servers, Discord servers, or commercial products is not allowed.High
  • 2.3Sharing personal information (doxxing) of anyone is a permanent ban offense.High

Gameplay & Fair Play

  • 3.1Cheating, hacking, or using modified clients that provide an unfair advantage is strictly banned.High
  • 3.2Exploiting bugs or glitches to gain items, money, or access restricted areas is prohibited. Report bugs to staff.High
  • 3.3Allowed mods include: Optifine, Sodium, Litematica (schematics), and Zoom mods. Any PvP enhancing mods are banned.Medium

Griefing & Stealing

  • 4.1Griefing or destroying other players' builds is strictly prohibited, even in unclaimed land.High
  • 4.2Stealing from unlocked chests is allowed in the Wilderness, but not in claimed territories.Medium

Economy & Trading

  • 5.1Scamming players in trades or auctions is a bannable offense.High
  • 5.2Real Money Trading (RMT) for in-game items or currency is strictly prohibited.High
